Introducing Our New Waste Reporting Website: A Step Towards a Cleaner Campus

In today's world, sustainability and waste management are more critical than ever. As our campus grows, so does the need for effective waste management solutions. To tackle this challenge, we're excited to introduce our brand-new Waste Reporting Website, a platform designed to empower our campus community to take action and contribute to a cleaner, greener environment.

Why a Waste Reporting Website?

Waste management on a college campus can be a daunting task,especially with a large and diverse community.Our Waste Reporting Website aims to address this by making it easy for everyone students, staff, and faculty to report waste issues in real-time.
